Frequently Asked Questions About CKO Kickboxing Emerson

Everything you need to know before your first class at Emerson's top-rated gym — from pricing and what to wear, to how we scale workouts for all fitness levels.

Do I need fitness experience to join?

Absolutely not. CKO Kickboxing Emerson welcomes complete beginners every day. Every workout is scaled to your current fitness level by our certified coaches, so you can start from wherever you are — no experience required.

How much does an Emerson membership cost?

We offer several membership options including unlimited group classes, personal training packages, and drop-in rates. Contact us or book a free intro class to learn about current pricing and find the plan that best fits your goals and budget.

What should I wear to my first class?

Wear comfortable athletic clothing and supportive sneakers (cross-trainers or flat-soled shoes work best). Bring a water bottle — we provide everything else you need for your first workout at our Emerson gym.

Is CKO Kickboxing safe for beginners and older adults?

Yes. Our training is fully scalable. Our coaches modify every movement to accommodate injuries, mobility limitations, and different fitness levels. We train members from age 14 to 70+ safely and effectively.

What areas does CKO Kickboxing Emerson serve?

Our gym is located at 500 Kinderkamack Rd in Emerson, NJ 07630. We serve members from Emerson, Westwood, Paramus, Oradell, Lakeville, Jordan, and the broader Bergen County area.

What does a typical class look like?

Each one-hour class includes a guided warm-up, skill or strength work, a high-intensity workout of the day, and a cool-down. Classes are led by certified coaches who explain every movement and ensure proper form throughout.
